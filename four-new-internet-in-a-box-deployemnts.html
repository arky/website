Four new Internet-in-a-Box Deployemnts

Braddock Gaskill - Thu 17 October 2013 -

Four new Internet-in-a-Box devices are now on their way to school deployments in Sierra Leone (2), Malawi, and Kiribati!

These join our prior deployments in Haiti (2), Sierra Leone, Pakistan, and Los Angeles.

In addition, the Unleashed Kids project is preparing to ship hard drives containing Internet-in-a-Box as a bonus with some of their 400 One Laptop Per Child XO-4 laptops now for sale. https://www.facebook.com/UnleashKids
