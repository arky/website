Contributing
############

We welcome your help in making our project a success. There are many ways to get involved and keep in touch.

1. Join our `mailing list <http://sgvhak.net/mailman/listinfo/iiab>`_

2. Join us on IRC:

   `#sgvhak <http://webchat.freenode.net/?channels=sgvhak>`_ on `freenode <https://www.freenode.net/>`_

3. Look through our `GitHub Issues <https://github.com/braddockcg/internet-in-a-box/issues>`_ and find a task that fits your skills:

   * Coding
   * Data curation
   * Documentation
   * Graphics design
   * Testing
   * Translation
   * Usability design
