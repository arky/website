Contributing

We welcome your help in making our project a success. There are many ways to get involved and keep in touch.

  1. Read our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  2. Join our technology and learning design mailing lists

  3. Explore our forums

  4. Join us on IRC live chat:

    #schoolserver on freenode

  5. Look through our GitHub Issues and find a task that fits your skills:

    • Coding
    • Data curation
    • Documentation
    • Graphics design
    • Testing
    • Translation
    • Usability design
